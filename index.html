About us

At Chespitos we believe in authenticity above all. With the healthiest ingredients imported directly from Mexico, we can assure you that the taste is exactly what you'd get if you were sitting at a restaurant in the middle of Tijuana. Our staff is passionate and excited to bring the perfect meal to your day. We pride ourselves on our quality experience, pleasing environment, and authentic mexican food.
